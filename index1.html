DESTINATIONS

San Juan

San Juan was known as "the surfing capital of the north". San Juan was perfect for those people who love waves and beach.

Christ the Redeemer

Christ the Redeemer was a big statue of Christ located at Hill top San Fernando, 2500 La Union. One of the most interesting in this place was that you can see the whole City.

View Deck

View Deck was become popular just this year. It was known because of the beautiful view of sunrise and sunset. You can also see the city lights at night.

Namacpacan Church

Namacpacan Church is located at Luna, La Union. It was a best place to go because it was quite and very peaceful.

Bahay na Bato

Bahay na Bato is one of the most popular tourist in Luna. The view here is so beautiful because it had made use of its own Luna product which is the pebbles to create such mesmerising place. You can only enjoy the pebbles around the house but also the art gallery it has inside of it.

Grape Farm

Grape Farm was usually located at Bauang La Union. It was located at the national highway.

Tangadan Falls

Falls is a place that is very rare to go to. We always go to beaches and pools to swim. But these Tangadan falls was worth of the walk, worth the money. Where you can see this amazing view as well as swim.

Urban Lights

This Urban Lights is located at Thunderbird Resort, it was inspired from Los Angeles Country Museum of art in California. It was a very nice place and beautiful view. Thunderbird also offers a lot of things.

Macho temple

Macho temple was a place for Chinese who is living in La Union. It was open for everyon who is interested to go there. It also have a beautiful view, and very suitable for peopel who loves photography.

PUGAD ADVENTURE

Pugad was one of the most popular swimming pool in Pugo. It also a lot of things like zipline, trekking, wall climbing and many more.
